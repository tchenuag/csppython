Lesson: Numbers and Operators
-----------------------------

Let's first review the four common math operators:
+-----------------+--------+----------------------+
| Operator        | Symbol | Example              |
+=================+========+======================+
| Addition        | ``+``  | ``3+7`` (result: 10) |
+-----------------+--------+----------------------+ 
| Subtraction     | ``-``  | ``5-8`` (result: -3) |

| Multiplication  | ``*``  | ``3*5`` (result: 15) |
+-----------------+--------+----------------------+
| Division        | ``/``  | ``6/2`` (result: 3)  |
